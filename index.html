About Me



Alexander Besancon is an accomplished and experienced military logistics leader with a Bachelors of History from Texas A&M University. During his time in the military he was able to build a cohesive team based on trust and mutual respect. This resulted in in his team reaching their organizations objectives in many places the United States and Kuwait. His belief in building a team based on trust and mutual respect enabled his team to mobilize a 65 vehicle convoy to support Hurricane Harvey relief efforts from Fort Riley, Kansas in six days. While Alexander was finding success as a logistics officer, he left the military to pursue a civilian career in the tech industry. He has always been curious with how technology and programing worked.
